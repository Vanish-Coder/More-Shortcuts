⌨ More Shortcuts
Collapse Group & Pause Media

Collapses the active tab's group and pauses any playing audio or video. (Recommended shortcut listed below, but you can choose your own.)

⌘⇧H / Ctrl⇧H on Windows
New Tab in New Group

Opens a new tab and immediately places it into its own fresh tab group. (Recommended shortcut listed below, but you can choose your own.)

⌘⇧G / Ctrl⇧G on Windows
Fake Cmd+Shift+T

Opens a random site from your list below. Every press is a surprise. (Recommended shortcut listed below, but you can choose your own.)

⌘⇧T / Ctrl⇧T on Windows
💡 Make sure to set up your shortcuts manually as well. Set it at chrome://extensions/shortcuts.
Unmute Current Tab

Unmutes the current tab in case it got stuck muted. (Recommended shortcut listed below, but you can choose your own.)

⌘⇧U / Ctrl⇧U on Windows
